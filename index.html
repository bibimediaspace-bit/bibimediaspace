About Me

The strategy behind the scroll.

I'm a digital marketing professional and creative strategist operating under the Bibi Media Space brand. My work sits at the intersection of compelling visuals, data-driven campaigns, and platform-native content — built to move audiences from discovery to conversion.

From brand identity systems to paid advertising funnels, I deliver end-to-end solutions. I design in Canva and Figma, produce short-form video with CapCut, run Meta and Google Ads, and leverage AI tools including ChatGPT and ElevenLabs to scale content without sacrificing quality.

My approach is always strategic: understand the brand, define the audience, then build content and campaigns that generate measurable results.
